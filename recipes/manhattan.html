Manhattan

Description

A Manhattan is a luscious, boozy cocktail comprised of bitters, cold water, sweet vermouth, and Rye whiskey. It is stirred, garnished with a luxardo cherry, and served in a coupe

Ingredients

  • -- Angostura Bitters
  • 1 oz. Sweet Vermouth
  • 2 oz. Rye Whiskey

Steps

  1. Add all Ingredients to a chilled mixing glass
  2. Place spoon in glass, followed by six or seven 1 inch cubes of ice
  3. Stir 35-40 times until body reaches optimal temperature and dilution
  4. Strain into a chilled coupe
  5. Garnish with a skewered luxardo cherry
  6. Serve
