Caipirinha

Description

A Caipirinha is the unofficial national cocktail of Brazil. It is made with cachaça, which is a freshly pressed sugarcane rum, lime, and a touch of sugar

Ingredients

  • 6 lime chunks
  • 1/8 oz. agave
  • 2 oz. cachaça

Steps

  1. Add all Ingredients to a shaking tin
  2. Using a muddler, press down into the tin repeatedly until the lime chunks have been mangled beyond recognition, about 6 times
  3. Fill shaking tin with 1 inch ice cubes, half of them cracked with an ice cracking spoon
  4. Shake hard for 10 seconds
  5. Dump entire contents of the shaking tin, unstrained, directly into a double rocks glass
  6. Serve
